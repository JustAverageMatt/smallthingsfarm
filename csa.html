Community supported agriculture

Share in the harvest.

A CSA share is a weekly box of whatever’s best in the beds — picked that morning, all season long. We’ll offer a small number of shares, and when they’re spoken for, they’re spoken for.

Please note

Orders will be accepted once Small Things Farm is up and running at full capacity. Nothing is for sale yet — the shares and pricing below are our working plan, so you know what’s coming.

The shares

Two sizes, one standard

SMALL SHARE
$30/week · est.
about $540 for the ~18-week season

6–8 items each week — right for one or two people. Salad greens most weeks, plus whatever the beds are proudest of: tomatoes, carrots, garlic, herbs.

FAMILY SHARE
$45/week · est.
about $810 for the ~18-week season

9–12 items each week for a household of three or more. The same standard, just more of it — and first crack at extras like cut flowers and honey.

Estimated pricing, in line with what small farms charge around both Hardin County and Columbus. Final prices are set before reservations open — joining the list commits you to nothing.

How it works

From reservation to table

STEP 1 — reserve
When reservations open, claim a share here. The number is limited — small enough that every box gets noticed.
STEP 2 — we grow
Ten intensive beds planted in succession, May to first frost, so the box changes every week.
STEP 3 — Saturday pickup
Collect your box at our market stand or at the farm gate — cut and pulled that same morning.

Reservations aren’t open yet

Once the farm is running at full capacity we’ll open a short reservation list right here, capped at the number of shares we can grow well.
